Order Processing


All of our orders are shipped from our Irish warehouse


Due to our warehouse location, any order within the EU Zone will NOT incur additional import taxes orduty upon delivery.


Modifications to Order

Due to system limitations, product modifications cannot be made once pre-orders are placed. Please verify your checkout cart before submitting the final order.

Some countries must pay applicable sales taxes at the time of purchase, such as VAT, GST or PST taxes.


Cancellations

Orders can only be cancelled on the same day by 12pm IST by emailing support@leveluploot.ie. For example, if you ordered between 1am-11am IST on Monday, you will only have until 12pm on the same day to email us to cancel the order.

Please make sure to include the name and order number of the order and reason for cancellation. Once your request has been received and cancelled, you will receive a cancellation email confirmation and you will be refunded using the same payment method. Please note the refund can take between 5-14 business days to show in your bank/credit card account, depending on your bank and country.

If you do not like to wait for refunds to be received in your bank/credit card account, it is important that you are 100% sure you want to place the order.


Change Of Address

For change of address, contact our support team at support@leveluploot.ie.

An address change must be requested for each order you would like to update. Updating your address in your account does not update the address for your existing orders. It is the customers responsibility to ensure the delivery address is correct before the order ships as we cannot change the delivery address after the order ships. We will not re-ship the product if the incorrect address is provided.


Delivery Delays

Please note that the delivery month shown on the product page is an estimate only. It is normal that there are production delays, especially given the circumstance with COVID-19 at the moment, but we will do our best to meet the approximate delivery month. As such, please be aware of possible delays if the preorder is intended as a future gift.


PAYMENT


Accepted Payments

The accepted cards for payment are Visa and Mastercard. If you have a Visa or Mastercard Debit card, this payment should work as well.


SHIPPING


Shipping from warehouse in Ireland

All orders with a shipping address in Europe will be shipped from our warehouse in the EU (Ireland).


Once you receive the tracking information, please monitor and make sure someone is available to receive and sign. We are not responsible for the delivery actions of couriers.


Some shipments to Asia and Africa may also incur duty and taxes.

Duty/custom fees are the responsibility of the customer. If you do not pay for these fees and the shipment is sent back, we will not re-ship the order to you. All shipping costs including failed delivery fees that are charged to us by the courier will be deducted from the order amount paid.


Modification to Shipping Address

It is possible to change the shipping address for all pre-orders before the estimated month of delivery. Please email support@leveluploot.ie or contact us on FB messenger for the address change. Please provide your order number, shipping address name, full address, email and phone number.


Tracking Information

All orders have tracking included to ensure peace of mind.


Customs/Duties Fees

Please note that the shipping cost we charge for the order excludes any customs/import fees. This extra charge will be the buyer's responsibility. Please check with your country's import rates for more information.

Duty/custom fees are the responsibility of the customer. If you do not pay for these fees and the shipment is sent back, we will not re-ship the order to you. All shipping costs including failed delivery fees that are charged to us by DHL/UPS will be deducted from the order amount paid.
